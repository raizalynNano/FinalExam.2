FLOWERS

ASTER

The Aster flowers carries a variety of meanings, but in general,the flower mainly represents love,wisdom, and faith. In Victorian culture, the Aster represents daintness,patience and charm. The Aster is the official birth flower for the September. When burnt, the odor emitted by the Aster is known to ward off serpents.

BEGONIA

Every flower has a special meaning behind it. The Begonia flower is no different and there are several meaning that are linked to this gorgeous flower. The Begonia flowers represents warning about misfortune,being cautious,dark thoughts,gratitude for something,individuality and uniqueness, justice and peace and harmonious communication.

CHRYSANTHEMUM

The Chrysanthemum is the flower of the month of November, and this beautiful flower comes with a wide variety of meanings all across the globe. In Asia the Chrysanthemum signifies life and rebirth, making them great gifts for birthdays and baby showers. In Europe the Chrysanthemum signifies an expression of sympathy. Chrysanthemum's are typically looked at as a flower that signifies respect and honor in America. In general,Chrysanthemum are believed to represents happiness, love, longevity and joy.
